# 📊 Student Social Media & Mental Health Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

The **Student Social Media & Mental Health Dashboard** is a Power BI project that explores how social media usage influences students' mental well-being. Using survey-based data, the dashboard uncovers patterns between screen time, study habits, sleep quality, physical activity, stress levels, and mental health scores.

The goal of this project is to transform raw data into actionable insights through interactive visualizations and data storytelling techniques.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

With the increasing use of social media among students, understanding its impact on mental health has become crucial. This project aims to answer questions such as:

- Does increased social media usage affect mental health?
- How do sleep patterns influence mental well-being?
- What relationship exists between physical activity and mental health?
- How do stress levels vary among students?
- Which social media platforms are most commonly used?

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Stress and Mental Health
Students with higher stress levels generally show lower mental health scores.

### 2. Sleep Patterns
Students who maintain healthy sleep schedules tend to report better mental health outcomes.

### 3. Social Media Usage
Higher daily social media usage is often associated with lower mental health scores.

### 4. Physical Activity
Regular physical activity appears to positively influence mental well-being.

### 5. Study Habits
Study hours and stress levels show notable relationships, highlighting the importance of balance in academic life.
